Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers for general contractors and project owners.

Is Summit Drywall licensed and insured?

Yes. Summit Drywall is fully licensed and insured for commercial drywall work throughout our service area in Utah.

What areas does Summit Drywall serve?

We serve Weber, Davis, Salt Lake, and Utah counties in Utah, along with surrounding areas on a case-by-case basis.

Does Summit Drywall handle residential or commercial projects?

We focus on mid-scale commercial projects, working as a subcontractor for general contractors on everything from ground-up construction to tenant improvements.

What drywall services does Summit Drywall offer?

We provide drywall hanging, mudding and taping, acoustic ceilings, and light-gauge steel framing.

Do you provide Level 5 finishes?

Yes. We finish to the level called for in the spec, including smooth Level 5 finishes for high-visibility commercial interiors.

How do I request a bid or quote from Summit Drywall?

Fill out the contact form on our website with your project location, scope, and timeline, and we'll get back to you within one business day.
